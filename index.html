Noah's Team

Noah's team consist of an ex-mercenary, Kotier Antires, and his brother Kirin Antires, the leader of Kirin Corporation. Together they fight and solve crimes that government tends to shy away from or profitting from. It is up to them to bring salvation to the people of the city during these dark times.
